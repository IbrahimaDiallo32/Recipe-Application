Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add wet ingredients and stir until smooth.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
